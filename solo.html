Electric Petals, the leading instrumental single from Stu Pender's forthcoming release Stillness In Motion, perfectly sets the table for Pender’s conjuring of awe inspired reverie. It’s floating guitar melodies ripple alongside brass accompaniment from Jaedon Alvira and Nat Ranson (Photay, Starchild and the New Romantic), anchored by the natural sounds from field recordings captured oceanside near Big Sur. Individually, Electric Petals is one of nine pieces totaling a greater reflection on finding simplicity in the chaos; pressing pause and taking time from our busy lives for effortless acts and experiences that bring us joy.

Stu Pender’s Electric Petals will be made available worldwide on digital formats December 16th. His deeply synesthetic full-length debut for Youngbloods, Stillness in Motion, will be made available January 27th.
