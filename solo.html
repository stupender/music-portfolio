stillness in motion is an upcoming album inspired by a visit to Big Sur, and later completed during the 2020 quarantine in Brooklyn, NY. Weaving guitar, voice, & field recordings, stillness in motion is a reflection of our time here on Earth, finding peace in how we choose to spend it, and that after the last note is played, nature will continue roaring on.
