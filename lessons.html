Hi, I'm Stu — Brooklyn-based guitarist with 15+ years of experience playing and teaching. Graduate of the SUNY Purchase Jazz Conservatory and a frequent collaborator with some of NYC's freshest talent. Student of the late renowned guitarist & composer, John Abercrombie.

I teach students of all ages and skill levels. Some topics we can cover include guitar technique, chords, learning songs, building a repertoire, ear training, music theory, & reading music.

I offer expertise in many styles such as jazz, classical, Brazilian, fingerstyle, blues, & contemporary guitar.

You have the option of lessons to your home, through my South Williamsburg studio, or via Skype. Reach out for rates & availability.
