Contact

Students of all ages and skill levels are encouraged to register. These are project based lessons directed by each student's individual goals and needs. Some topics we may cover include music theory, guitar fundamentals and technique, repertoire, ear training, recording, music production and the creative process. I offer experience in many styles such as jazz, hip hop, classical, Brazilian, fingerstyle, folk, blues, and contemporary guitar.

In order to help make these courses affordable to as many people as possible, I'm offering flexible rates based on a choice of either private lessons or submission based feedback. These will all take place remotely for the time being. May creativity bring us all a little closer together!
