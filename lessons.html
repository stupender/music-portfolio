Hi, I'm Stu Pender — Brooklyn-based guitarist with 15+ years of experience performing and teaching. Graduate of the SUNY Purchase Jazz Conservatory and former student of renowned guitarist & composer John Abercrombie.

Students of all ages and skill levels are encouraged to register. Some topics we will cover include music theory, guitar fundamentals & technique, repertoire, ear training, and the creative process.

I offer experience in many styles such as jazz, classical, Brazilian, fingerstyle, folk, blues & contemporary guitar.

In order to help make these courses affordable to as many people as possible, I'm offering flexible rates based on a choice of either private or group lessons. These will all take place remotely for the time being. May creativity bring you peace during these turbulent times.
